About Youth For LeBron James Foundation

The Youth For LeBron James Foundation is dedicated to empowering and inspiring the youth through education and sports. Founded by LeBron James, the foundation aims to provide opportunities and resources for young people to achieve their dreams.

Our Projects

We have various projects focused on education, sports, and community development. Our initiatives include scholarship programs, sports camps, and mentorship programs.
